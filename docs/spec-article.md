# Article Page Spec — /[slug]

## Frontmatter Template

```yaml
---
title: "[keyword] — [value] [year]"  # <60 chars
slug: "keyword-in-english"
date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
category: "עם ילדים" | "כשר" | "הפלגות" | "טיולים מאורגנים"
destination: "שם יעד בעברית"
destinationSlug: "destination-slug"
country: "שם מדינה בעברית"
countrySlug: "country-slug"
excerpt: "2 sentences describing the article"
keywords: ["keyword1", "keyword2", "keyword3"]
author: "חופשה היום"
tldr: "2-3 direct sentences answering the search query"
factBox:
  flightTime: "X:XX שעות"
  currency: "שם מטבע (CODE)"
  language: "שפה"
  bestSeason: "חודש-חודש"
  dailyBudget: "$XXX-XXX לזוג/למשפחה (משוער, נכון ל-2026)"
  temperature: "XX-XX°C"
faq:
  - q: "שאלה?"
    a: "תשובה של 3-4 משפטים מינימום"
---
```

## Content Requirements

- **Word count**: 1,000-2,000 words (body only, not frontmatter)
- **H2 headings**: Phrased as questions the reader asks ("כמה ימים צריך ב...?")
- **TL;DR box**: 2-3 sentences with facts, numbers, keyword. Appears first.
- **Fact Box**: All 6 fields filled. Budget always as range.
- **FAQ**: 3-5 real questions. Each answer: 3-4 sentences minimum with specifics.
- **Closing**: Every article ends with "לסיכום:" blockquote before CTA box.

## Budget Table Rules

- min of total = sum of all row minimums
- max of total = sum of all row maximums
- TL;DR budget = Fact Box budget = body table budget = FAQ budget (all identical)
- Verify before commit: no number appears in two different versions in the article

## Internal Links Required

- Link to country page (e.g., /greece)
- Link to category page (e.g., /with-kids)
- 2+ related articles in same country or category
- City page if relevant (e.g., /athens)

## SEO

- Primary keyword: 3-5 times (H1, TL;DR, one H2, body, FAQ)
- Variations: 10-15 times naturally
- Title tag: <60 chars
- Meta description: <155 chars
- Schema: Article + FAQ + BreadcrumbList (auto-generated by [slug]/page.tsx)

## Hero Image

- Prompt starts with "Photorealistic photograph of"
- VIVID suffix appended (see CLAUDE.md rule 4)
- Generated via `scripts/generate-images.ts` (direct Gemini API, NOT MCP)
- Compressed with sharp: max 1200px wide, JPEG 85%, target <150KB
- Saved to `public/images/posts/[slug].jpg`
